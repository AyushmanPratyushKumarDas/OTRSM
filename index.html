old history of ଓଡିଶା

The history of Odisha, an eastern Indian state, dates back to antiquity. It was known by several names such as Kalinga, Utkal, and Odra. The ancient Odra desa or Ordesa was limited to the valley of Mahanadi and to the lower course of Subarnarekha River. By the time of the Mahabharata, Kalinga, Utkal, and Odra had entered into Aryan polity as powerful kingdoms.
